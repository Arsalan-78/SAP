THE BRIEF

What the agent
actually does

Right now, someone on your AP team opens each invoice, finds the matching purchase order in SAP, checks the numbers line by line, and decides whether to post it or chase it. This agent does that same check — n8n receives the invoice, FastAPI and OCR pull out the fields, a LangGraph agent matches it against the PO in SAP, and it either posts the invoice, explains exactly what doesn't line up, or sends it to a person with that explanation attached.

Matches every invoice against its SAP purchase order automatically
Writes a plain-English reason whenever something doesn't match
Posts clean invoices to SAP without anyone touching them
Escalates anything uncertain to a person, with context attached

CASE FILES

The five components,
in plain terms

app/main.py
FastAPI backend

Receives the invoice from n8n, runs it through the pipeline, and returns a structured decision. One endpoint: /process-invoice.

app/agent.py
LangGraph agent

Runs the 3-way match, decides to approve or flag, and writes the explanation a human would read.

match_po approve explain_mismatch
app/ocr_extractor.py
OCR extractor

Tesseract and pdf2image turn the PDF into text; parsing pulls out invoice number, PO number, vendor, and total.

app/sap_connector.py
SAP connector

Talks to API_PURCHASEORDER_PROCESS_SRV over OData and posts invoices. Ships with realistic mock POs, MOCK_MODE = True by default.

n8n/invoice-workflow.json
n8n workflow

Ready to import: catches the webhook, calls FastAPI, routes on the decision, sends the notification email.

THE AUDIT TRAIL

What happens between
upload and posting

STEP 01 · INTAKE
Invoice arrives

An n8n webhook or email trigger catches the PDF and forwards it to FastAPI.

STEP 02 · READING
Fields get extracted

OCR pulls the text out, then parsing structures it into invoice number, PO number, vendor, and total.

STEP 03 · JUDGMENT
The agent checks it against SAP

It pulls the matching PO and runs the 3-way match, then routes to auto-approve, explain-the-mismatch, or escalate.

STEP 04 · OUTCOME
SAP is updated, someone is told

Clean invoices post automatically. Anything else reaches a person by email, with the reason already written.
